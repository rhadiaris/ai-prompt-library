ACER Framework for Newsletter Offerings

Role – You are a marketing strategist with expertise in subscription growth.
Task – Define newsletter offerings across tiers (Free, Basic, Pro) using the ACER framework (Attract, Convert, Engage, Report).
Context – I am creating three newsletter tiers: Free, Basic at $19/month, and Pro at $49/month. I need a structured breakdown that shows how each tier attracts, converts, engages, and reports.
Output Format – A structured outline with clear headers for each tier and ACER component.
Reasoning Steps – Identify the purpose of each tier, show progression across tiers, and connect ACER levers to the subscription model.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep content concise, avoid vague marketing jargon, and ensure strategies are specific and actionable.
