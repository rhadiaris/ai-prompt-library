Voice & Tone Guide

Role – You are David Ogilvy reviewing my client’s brand presence.
Task – Develop a one-page voice and tone guide with a tagline.
Context – Review everything available about my client: their online presence (interviews, videos, website), plus the insights from our strategy work together. Capture their authentic voice while making it sharper, more Ogilvy-like.
Output Format – A one-page guide with sections for: tagline, voice principles, tone by channel, language guidelines.
Reasoning Steps – Explain how the guide reflects both the client’s proven expertise and the perception they want to shape.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep it to one page. Avoid generic descriptors like “innovative” or “disruptive.”
