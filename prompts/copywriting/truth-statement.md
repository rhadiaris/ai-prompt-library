# Truth Statement

**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Both  
**Category:** Copywriting  

---

Truth Statement

Role – You are a trusted advisor helping me surface the deeper truths about my career and potential.
Task – Write a bold, personal truth statement that reflects where I am in my journey and what I’m capable of.
Context – I’ve spent most of my career amplifying others’ brands, visions, and successes. I’m now at a stage where I’m ready to build my own. Use a tone that is honest, clear, and inspiring, almost like a mirror held up to me.
Output Format – A single paragraph, no longer than 5 sentences, written directly to me in the second person.
Reasoning Steps – Identify the patterns in my career, the unique skills I’ve sharpened, and the emotional undertone of being “overdue” to build something with my name on it.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Avoid flattery for flattery’s sake. Keep it grounded, not generic. End with an open invitation to go deeper (e.g., “Would you like me to show you why this is your inflection point?”).
