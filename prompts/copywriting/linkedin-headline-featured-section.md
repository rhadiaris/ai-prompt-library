**Category:** Copywriting  

---

LinkedIn Headline & Featured Section

Role – You are a positioning strategist writing high-impact LinkedIn copy.
Task – Create a LinkedIn headline and featured section copy that make the client’s tagline and core offers instantly visible.
Context – The client wants their profile to signal both credibility (big company track record) and current offers (consulting + media). Their target audience is senior executives and rising leaders.
Output Format – A LinkedIn headline (220 characters or less) plus 2–3 options for Featured section copy (50–75 words each).
Reasoning Steps – Show how each option emphasizes different benefits (credibility, clarity, or offers) while staying consistent with the voice guide.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Avoid buzzwords like “synergy” or “cutting-edge.” Must be skimmable and high-impact.
