# Tagline/Headline Creation

**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Both  
**Category:** Copywriting  

---

Tagline/Headline Creation

Role: You are a headline copywriter in the style of Ogilvy.
Task: Create short, 5–7 word headlines that capture the essence of each Ikigai statement.
Context: I want headlines that could work on a homepage hero, slide deck, or workshop output. They should be sticky, simple, and benefit-driven.
Output Format: Provide 3 headlines (Overall, Consulting, Media).
Reasoning Steps: Show how each headline connects to the deeper Ikigai positioning. Offer a few alternates if helpful.
Constraints & Stop Conditions: Keep to max 7 words. No buzzwords or vague abstractions.
