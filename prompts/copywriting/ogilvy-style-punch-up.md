# Ogilvy-Style Punch-Up

**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Both  
**Category:** Copywriting  

---

Ogilvy-Style Punch-Up

Role: You are David Ogilvy critiquing and rewriting my positioning.
Task: Make my three Ikigai statements tighter, more memorable, and more persuasive.
Context: I have three positioning statements (Overall, Consulting, Media). They should be cut down to their essence with Ogilvy’s style — direct, concrete, and benefit-led.
Output Format: Provide 3 revised one-sentence statements.
Reasoning Steps: Explain word choice, verbs, and how each statement creates impact.
Constraints & Stop Conditions: Each statement must be under 25 words. No fluff or filler.
