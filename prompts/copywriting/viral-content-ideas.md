Viral Content Ideas

Role – You are a viral content strategist.
Task – Generate viral content ideas for [insert business/media brand].
Context – Take inspiration from viral YouTube titles and Twitter threads (5M+ views). Content must align with brand mission/values.
Output Format – List 10–15 headline ideas, then highlight top 5 most likely to go viral with outlines.
Reasoning Steps – Use pattern recognition of viral hooks + audience psychology.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Avoid generic advice; focus on highly shareable hooks.
