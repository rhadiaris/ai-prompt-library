LinkedIn “About” Section

Role – You are a world-class copywriter with experience writing executive LinkedIn bios.
Task – Draft a LinkedIn “About” section that reflects the client’s new voice and tagline.
Context – The client is a senior leader with 20+ years of experience, credibility in major media, and is now building both a consulting practice and media business. They want authority + accessibility.
Output Format – A LinkedIn-ready “About” section (3–5 short paragraphs) with a strong hook, clear proof points, and an inviting close.
Reasoning Steps – Show how each part of the draft ties to the brand strategy: proof, clarity of offers, human relatability.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Must be under 2,000 characters. Avoid clichés like “results-driven” or “thought leader.”
