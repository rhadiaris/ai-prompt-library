# Ambition - The 10-Year Vision Clarity Test


---

Role:
Act like a world-class strategic consultant and narrative architect.

Objective:
Distill my company’s 10-year ambition into one clear, credible line that connects purpose, impact, and identity. This prompt is for ChatGPT in the browser (not the API).

Inputs (provide below, in plain text):
1) Mission (why we exist):
2) Product category (what we make/offer):
3) Customer promise (commitment to users/customers):
4) Current positioning tagline/sentence (how we describe ourselves today):

Task:
Write a 1–2 sentence vision statement that begins with “In 10 years, we will…” followed by a 3-bullet rationale labeled Purpose, Proof, Payoff.

Process (follow these steps in order before writing):
1) Extract the core intent from the Mission in ≤12 words.
2) Map Product category to the human problem it solves (not features).
3) Translate the Customer promise into a measurable change for customers and the market.
4) Compare stated purpose vs. current positioning; note gaps or contradictions.
5) Identify what is believable (evidence we can marshal), differentiated (what others can’t credibly claim), and motivating (emotion + stakes).
6) Draft 2–3 candidate phrasings; stress-test for clarity, specificity, and plain language.
7) Select the strongest option and refine to one line (or two, if essential).

Output Format:
In 10 years, we will [specific change in the world] by [distinct approach/identity] so that [who] can [outcome].
• Purpose: [why this matters to people/society]
• Proof: [credible signals, traction, capabilities, advantages that make it believable]
• Payoff: [measurable customer/market result and strategic upside]

Constraints:
- Exclude marketing slogans, buzzwords, and internal jargon. Use simple, human words.
- Be specific (quantify or qualify outcomes where reasonable). Avoid vague claims.
- Minimum editing needed: deliver a clean, final statement with the 3-bullet rationale.

Quality Bar & Self-Check (do before finalizing):
- Is the statement crisp, differentiated, and emotionally resonant?
- Would a smart outsider understand and believe it without extra context?
- Replace any abstractions with concrete nouns/verbs.
