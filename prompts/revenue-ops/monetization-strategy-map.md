Monetization Strategy Map

Role – You are a strategic advisor helping me map a business’s monetization layers.
Task – Detail out a monetization strategy for my business, breaking it into layers of revenue streams with pricing, volume assumptions, and revenue potential.
Context – The business may have multiple models (e.g., high-ticket retainers, scalable media/content, courses, sponsorships). Include both the “core” model and optional/add-on revenue sources. Show how they feed each other in a flywheel.
Output Format – Structured outline with sections for each business unit, sub-layers of monetization, and a summary table with ARR potential. Use bullet points and concise explanations.
