# Biz Dev Company Deep Research ([COMPANY] example)


---

Conduct deep research on the company [COMPANY] and answer these 13 questions with your findings:

Answer these questions using the full canonical output of the vision framework: 

1. Big Picture Alignment
Purpose: Understand what “winning” means and where alignment breaks. Most teams don’t have a strategy problem — they have a shared-understanding problem.

Q1. What are your top three growth priorities for the next 12 months?

Q2. Which one feels most urgent and least clear?

Q3. If we achieved just one outcome together, what would make this a clear win?

2. Product & Market Clarity
Purpose: Reveal confusion in the product story, customer understanding, or GTM motion. If the product story isn’t simple, adoption will always lag.

Q4. How simply can your team explain your product in one sentence?

Q5. Where do customers slow down, hesitate, or ask clarifying questions?

Q6. What differentiator makes your product unavoidable for your ideal buyer?

3. Brand & Positioning Clarity
Purpose: Identify whether your brand helps or hurts your strategy. A brand is a decision system. When it’s unclear, everything downstream is unclear.

Q7. What belief should every customer, partner, and employee share about your brand?

Q8. What does your brand promise that competitors don’t?

Q9. Where does your messaging feel diluted, outdated, or inconsistent?

4. Execution Clarity
Purpose: Diagnose execution drag and clarity friction. Rhythm replaces chaos. Clarity creates rhythm.

Q10. How do you decide what gets built, shipped, or prioritized each week?

Q11. Which meetings create clarity — and which generate noise?

Q12. Where does work slow down because of unclear roles, steps, or decisions?

5. Ultimate Outcome
Q13. If this diagnostic gave you clarity in your business, what would change in the next 90 days?
