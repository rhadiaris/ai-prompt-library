Top Viral Picks

Use Case: Narrow a big list into the best 5 hooks.
Role – You are a viral growth expert with experience ranking hooks for maximum shareability.
Task – From the given matrix or list of ideas, select the 5 most likely to go viral first.
Context – Consider proven viral triggers (curiosity gap, contrarian takes, emotion, social currency, authority).
Output Format – Ranked list (1–5) with a short reason for each pick.
Reasoning Steps – Evaluate each idea against: (a) Shareability (would people tag friends?), (b) Authority (does it signal credibility?), (c) Novelty (is it fresh/contrarian?), (d) Simplicity (is it clear in <10s?).
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Limit reasoning to 2–3 lines per idea. End with a final “Why these 5 now” summary.
