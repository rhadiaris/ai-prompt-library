Viral Content Outline

Use Case: Expand a headline into a detailed script/outline.
Role – You are a viral storyteller skilled at scripting YouTube/Twitter/LinkedIn content.
Task – Turn a single viral headline into a content outline with 5–7 beats.
Context – The headline is: [INSERT HEADLINE]. Audience is [TARGET AUDIENCE]. Platform is [PLATFORM]. Use pacing that balances authority, story, and action steps.
Output Format – Numbered outline with Hook, Setup, Main Points (3–5), Emotional Beat, Payoff, CTA.
Reasoning Steps – Identify: (a) What emotion or curiosity will pull the audience in? (b) What proof/story builds credibility? (c) What takeaway gives them value worth sharing?
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep outlines <250 words. Must include a strong Hook and a CTA at the end.
