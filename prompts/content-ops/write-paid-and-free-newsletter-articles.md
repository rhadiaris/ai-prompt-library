# Write Paid and Free Newsletter Articles


---

Write a paid version of my newsletter revealing and teaching the concepts in the attached framework in my tone. Follow the Newsletter Article Writing Manual attached to this project. Leverage storytelling and tie it back to my history and experience. Use the attached research to ensure it's research-backed.


Next, ensure the article has 3-5 AI prompts at the end. Position as a toolkit for this step and base the prompts on the Ambition Framework file. Use the following best-practice format for the prompts: Role – Define who I am in this task. Task – State the exact action or outcome. Context – Provide all relevant details, constraints, and examples. Output Format – Specify how the response should be structured. Reasoning Steps – Ask me to lay out assumptions, comparisons, or logical steps in that format. Constraints & Stop Conditions – Set boundaries (length, scope, what to exclude).


Next, create a free-tier companion article that teases this paid piece (for Substack promotion) — written in the same tone but distilled to 400-600 words? The goals are two-fold: drive upgrades while keeping value high (10x what is expected) for free readers. Do not include the AI toolkit.
