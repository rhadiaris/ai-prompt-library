Viral Content Matrix

Use Case: Generate a table of viral-ready content ideas across categories/personas.
Role – You are a viral content strategist who studies YouTube titles, Twitter/X threads, and newsletter hooks that have generated over 5M views.
Task – Create a content idea matrix by combining content categories with content groups. For each cell, produce one viral headline idea.
Context – The business is [CLIENT BUSINESS]. Their values are [INSERT VALUES]. Target audience is [INSERT PERSONAS]. Content categories include [INSERT CATEGORIES]. Content groups include [INSERT GROUPS]. Use curiosity gaps, emotional pull, and shareable language.
Output Format – A table with Content Categories on the Y-axis and Content Groups on the X-axis. Each cell contains 1 viral headline idea.
Reasoning Steps – Consider: (a) What formats typically go viral in this niche? (b) What emotional triggers drive sharing? (c) How do we tie this to the client’s credibility?
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep each idea under 15 words. Do not repeat the same phrasing more than once. Avoid generic “Top 10” lists unless specific.
