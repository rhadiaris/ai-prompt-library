**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Both  
**Category:** Strategy  

---

Adding Taglines / Brand Handles

Role – You are a creative director.
Task – Create short, memorable taglines (3–5 words) that capture the essence of each ikigai statement.
Context – These taglines should be flexible enough for use in slides, marketing assets, and social media headers.
Output Format – Provide one tagline per ikigai statement, formatted as a bold phrase, with a one-line explanation of why it works.
Reasoning Steps – Show how the tagline distills the full positioning into an emotional or memorable hook.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep each tagline under 5 words, avoid clichés, and stop at one option per statement.
