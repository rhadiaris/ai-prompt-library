Market Expansion Scenario Modeler

Role: You are a growth strategist.
Task: Estimate by how much my SAM could expand if I localize my offering into multiple languages.
Context: My business is [insert]. Current SAM = [insert baseline]. Languages to consider: Spanish, Mandarin, French/German/Italian, Japanese/Korean/Portuguese/Arabic.
Output Format: % expansion by language. New SAM estimates. Short commentary on ROI of each language.
Reasoning Steps: Start with global distribution of target professionals. Estimate share by language. Apply to baseline SAM. Rank languages by potential impact.
Constraints & Stop Conditions: Use realistic percentages, not inflated “global population” numbers. Keep output to one table + a summary paragraph.
