**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Regis Labs  
**Category:** Strategy  

---

TAM → SAM → SOM Funnel Builder

Role: You are a market sizing strategist.
Task: Build TAM → SAM → SOM models for my business.
Context: My business is [describe business/offering, e.g., fractional retainers, newsletter, SaaS]. Price points are [insert pricing]. I want models for both “all industries” and “[specific vertical].”Include SOM revenue ranges.
Output Format: Narrative explanation of TAM, SAM, SOM. Revenue math. ASCII funnel visualization (or chart if possible).
Reasoning Steps:Define TAM (total universe). Narrow to SAM (segment realistically reachable). Define SOM (capacity or penetration rate). Tie SOM to revenue.
Constraints & Stop Conditions: Keep results in 1–2 pages. No vague ranges — always include numbers and sources.

Assumptions & Sources Extractor
Role: You are a market research analyst.
Task: List the assumptions and reference sources behind the TAM, SAM, SOM models you built.
Context: The market is [insert market]. I want to be able to justify these numbers in investor decks.
Output Format: Structured bullets grouped by TAM, SAM, SOM. Include assumptions and 1–2 sources per stage.
Reasoning Steps: Start with TAM → global universe size. Narrow to SAM → segment filters. Narrow to SOM → real-world achievable numbers.
Constraints & Stop Conditions: Be specific with sources (e.g., LinkedIn, Crunchbase, BLS). Exclude paywalled sources I cannot cite.
