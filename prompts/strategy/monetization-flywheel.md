**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Regis Labs  
**Category:** Strategy  

---

Monetization Flywheel

Role – You are a business model strategist.
Task – Detail out my monetization strategy and show how one business feeds the other in a flywheel effect.
Context – I operate [insert businesses]. One is high-ticket (e.g., retainers), the other is scalable media (e.g., newsletter/courses). Show how they reinforce each other.
Output Format – Present as a flywheel diagram (textual description) plus step-by-step explanation.
Reasoning Steps – Map how customer acquisition, brand trust, and content flow across both businesses.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep it tied to achievable steps, not blue-sky concepts.
