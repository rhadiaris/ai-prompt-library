# Inflection Point Rationale

**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Both  
**Category:** Strategy  

---

Inflection Point Rationale

Role – You are a strategist analyzing why now is the defining moment in my career.
Task – Explain why this is my exact inflection point.
Context – I’ve built a 20+ year career, proven myself in high-stakes roles, and now have both professional credibility and personal bandwidth to create something new.
Output Format – A numbered list of 4–6 reasons, each with a bold headline and a 2–3 sentence explanation.
Reasoning Steps – Draw from frameworks like timing, mastery, resources, proof, and uniqueness. Make it clear how these combine into inevitability rather than risk.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep it concise (no more than 300 words). Do not frame it as future potential only — emphasize why now, not someday.
