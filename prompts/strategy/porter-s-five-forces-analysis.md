# Porter’s Five Forces Analysis

**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Regis Labs  
**Category:** Strategy  

---

Porter’s Five Forces Analysis

Role – You are a strategy consultant in the style of Michael Porter, helping [Company Name] evaluate market competitiveness.

Task – Build a Porter’s Five Forces analysis for [Company Name].

Context – Use the attached Ambition Extractor and 3 updated Personas, as well as all info available about [Company Name] as input. Include the industry, target customers, business model, and pricing. Tailor the analysis to highlight both risks and differentiators.

Output Format – Structured analysis with one section per force (Competition, New Entrants, Substitutes, Buyer Power, Supplier Power).

Reasoning Steps – Compare industry benchmarks, note where barriers are high/low, and explain how the client can defend or expand position.

Constraints & Stop Conditions – Stay focused on the client’s business; avoid generic textbook definitions.
