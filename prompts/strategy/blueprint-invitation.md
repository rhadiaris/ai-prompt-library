# Blueprint Invitation

**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Both  
**Category:** Strategy  

---

Blueprint Invitation

Role – You are a mentor offering to turn insight into action.
Task – Invite me to take the next step by outlining what a personalized blueprint could look like.
Context – After surfacing truths and explaining the inflection point, the client may feel momentum but also uncertainty. The goal is to move them toward clarity and action.
Output Format – 1–2 sentences as a closing invitation, positioning the blueprint as the natural next move.
Reasoning Steps – Reinforce that insight without execution is incomplete. Frame the blueprint as a way to channel energy into structure.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep it short, not a full blueprint itself — just the offer of one.
