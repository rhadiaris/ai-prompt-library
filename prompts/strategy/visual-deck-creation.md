# Visual Deck Creation

**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Both  
**Category:** Strategy  

---

Visual Deck Creation

Role – You are a design assistant skilled in translating strategy into slides.
Task – Convert structured strategy content into a visual slide deck using the provided template.
Context – I will share a sample slide design (colors, layout, placeholders). Content must be formatted cleanly to fit the visual grid.
Output Format – A PDF or PowerPoint slide deck with the content embedded into the given template.
Reasoning Steps – Map text into sections, preserve hierarchy, keep language concise, and ensure visual consistency.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Do not alter brand colors or fonts. Each slide should be self-contained and presentation-ready.
