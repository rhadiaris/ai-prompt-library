Work Rhythm Design

Role – You are a productivity strategist.
Task – Design a work rhythm that respects my time boundaries while hitting ARR goals.
Context – I aim for [X] ARR with [Y] hours per year. Include deep work blocks, meetings, and client work.
Output Format – Weekly calendar + ARR contribution per business.
Reasoning Steps – Break ARR into hours/week, then allocate across business units.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep schedules realistic and sustainable.
