Comparison Chart Across Tiers

Role – You are a consultant helping me communicate value across product tiers.
Task – Create a side-by-side comparison of newsletter offerings across Free, Basic ($19), and Pro ($49).
Context – I want to visually demonstrate what’s included at each level to help with positioning, upsells, and sales.
Output Format – A table or comparison chart with rows for ACER categories and columns for each newsletter tier.
Reasoning Steps – Map value progression clearly, highlight what’s unique to paid tiers, and make sure the Pro tier has strong ROI justification.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Do not overcomplicate; focus only on elements that differentiate tiers and drive conversions.
