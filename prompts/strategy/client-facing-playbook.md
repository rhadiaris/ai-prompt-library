Client-Facing Playbook

Role – You are a business advisor building repeatable frameworks for scaling newsletters.
Task – Turn the ACER breakdown into a reusable playbook format that clients could adapt to their own Free/Basic/Pro models.
Context – My clients may have different price points or communities, but the framework should remain portable.
Output Format – A template with fill-in-the-blank guidance under each ACER component for each tier.
Reasoning Steps – Generalize from my example while preserving the structure so others can slot in their own tiers and details.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep language flexible and modular so clients can adapt it to their own products.
