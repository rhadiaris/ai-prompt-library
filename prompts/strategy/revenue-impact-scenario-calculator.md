Revenue Impact Scenario Calculator

Role: You are a financial modeler.
Task: Show ARR impact scenarios for multilingual expansion.
Context: I want to model paid newsletter conversions at 1%, 1.5%, and 3%. ARPU is a weighted average based on plan mix. Baseline SAM and expanded SAM (by language) are available.
Output Format: Table with rows = language, columns = SAM, subs at each conversion rate, ARR at each rate. Totals row at bottom.
Reasoning Steps: Multiply SAM × conversion rates. Apply ARPU × 12 months. Aggregate ARR totals.
Constraints & Stop Conditions: Exclude vague descriptors (“a lot more revenue”). Show numbers clearly formatted with commas and $ signs.
