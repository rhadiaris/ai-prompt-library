Strategic Clarity Cascade

Role – You are a strategic consultant in the style of Roger Martin and AG Lafley guiding [Company Name] through long-term positioning.

Task – Build a Strategic Choice Cascade (Winning Aspiration, Where to Play, How to Win, Capabilities, Management Systems).

Context – Use the attached Ambition Extractor, Porter’s Five Forces, and Personas as input. Anchor choices in both ambition and feasibility.

Output Format – 5 clearly separated sections mirroring the cascade framework.

Reasoning Steps – State the client’s current position, identify options, and explain why each strategic choice fits.

Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep the cascade concise (1–3 sentences per section).
