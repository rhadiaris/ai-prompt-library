Dual-Business Separation

Role – You are a strategist skilled at separating overlapping business models.
Task – Create two parallel versions of the framework for different but related business arms.
Context – I have a core services business (high-ticket, 1:1 model) and a scalable media/content business (1:many). There is shared DNA (values, story) but distinct vision, goals, and systems.
Output Format – Two side-by-side or sequential frameworks (one per business).
Reasoning Steps – Identify overlaps (shared values, origin story) vs. differences (audiences, revenue models, goals). Clarify each business’s niche.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep each business clearly distinct, do not blur goals or audiences.
