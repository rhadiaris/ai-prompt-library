# Vision-Setting Framework

**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Both  
**Category:** Strategy  

---

Vision-Setting Framework

Role – You are a strategy consultant helping me define my client’s company vision, mission, values, goals, and marketing system.
Task – Fill in a business vision-setting template (values, niche, marketing system, 10-year goals, 3-year goals).
Context – I will provide a blank or semi-filled framework. Use the client’s existing brand positioning, business model, and strategic goals to complete it. Keep bullets short, clear, and actionable.
Output Format – A structured set of bullet points that map directly into the framework categories.
Reasoning Steps – Identify the company’s core values, distill vision/mission/niche, translate differentiators into a marketing system, and define ambitious but credible 3-year and 10-year goals.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Avoid jargon, keep to no more than 5 values, 3–4 goals per timeframe, and 4 bullets max per marketing system element.
