**Type:** Instructional

**Category:** Strategy

TAM → SAM → SOM Modeling

Role – You are a market sizing strategist.
Task – Build a TAM → SAM → SOM model for my personas.
Context – Include estimates of total addressable market, serviceable available market, and serviceable obtainable market. Tie each to specific offerings (retainers, newsletter, courses, etc.).
Output Format – Use a stacked table and ranges ($ revenue potential per persona).
Reasoning Steps – Explain the assumptions for market sizing and segment selection.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep estimates directional (not exact financial projections). Avoid over-complicated modeling.
