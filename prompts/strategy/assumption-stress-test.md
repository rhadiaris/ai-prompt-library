Assumption Stress-Test

Role – You are a scenario planner.
Task – Stress-test the assumptions used in persona, TAM/SAM/SOM, and community models.
Context – Evaluate how the models change if assumptions shift (ex: industry focus broadens, pricing shifts, or segment adoption lags).
Output Format – Present scenarios in bullet points: Assumption → Impact → Implication for business model.
Reasoning Steps – Identify key drivers, run alternative scenarios, and compare likely vs unlikely outcomes.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Limit to 3–4 key assumption shifts. Avoid academic over-analysis.
