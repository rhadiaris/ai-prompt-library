Community Lifecycle Funnel

Role – You are an ecosystem designer.
Task – Create a lifecycle funnel that shows how different persona-based communities evolve and interconnect.
Context – Map how entry-level personas grow into advanced ones (e.g., operators → founders → executives). Show how each group both consumes value and feeds the others. Tie this explicitly to my business offerings (1:1 retainers vs 1:many content).
Output Format – Funnel diagram (conceptual or text-based) + explanation of interconnections.
Reasoning Steps – Use career progression and industry lifecycle patterns to explain movement between personas.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Avoid unnecessary complexity. Keep to 3–4 stages max with clear arrows and pathways.
