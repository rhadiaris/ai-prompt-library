Client Instructions

Role – You are a client success guide.
Task – Explain to the client how to import their LinkedIn data into Airtable and set it up as a CRM.
Context – The client will have a prepared CSV with personas and industries already tagged. They need step-by-step guidance to create the base, assign field types, and set up views for Persona, Industry, Priority, and Status.
Output Format – Deliver a structured checklist with numbered steps and Airtable field-type mappings.
Reasoning Steps – Show why each field type is chosen and how the views make the CRM usable.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep the instructions non-technical and easy to follow, no code.
