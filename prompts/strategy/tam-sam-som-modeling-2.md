TAM → SAM → SOM Modeling

Role – You are a market sizing strategist.
Task – Build TAM, SAM, and SOM models for my business.
Context – My business is [insert business description]. Provide market sizing first across all industries, then narrowed to [industry focus]. Include revenue ranges and sources.
Output Format – Use a stacked model view with TAM → SAM → SOM breakdowns, revenue estimates, and a short narrative explanation.
Reasoning Steps – Show assumptions, data sources, and comparisons used to estimate each level.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep results focused on realistic, actionable numbers. Avoid overinflated projections.
