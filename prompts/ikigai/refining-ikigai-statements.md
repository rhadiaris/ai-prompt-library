**Type:** Instructional

Refining Ikigai Statements

Role – You are a positioning strategist and editor.
Task – Take draft ikigai statements and make them sharper, shorter, and more resonant.
Context – The statements will be used for a personal brand, consulting business, and content platform. They should be differentiated but cohesive.
Output Format – Provide 3–5 revised statements in the format: “I help [audience] achieve [outcome] by [core strength], so they can [deeper impact].” Highlight differences in audience focus.
Reasoning Steps – Show how each option targets different personas or business lines, and explain why specific word choices make it stronger.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Avoid jargon, keep each statement under ~40 words, and stop at a maximum of 5 variations.
