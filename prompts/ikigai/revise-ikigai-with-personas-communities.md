# Revise Ikigai with Personas/Communities

**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Both  
**Category:** Ikigai  

---

Revise Ikigai with Personas/Communities

Role: You are my strategic positioning partner.
Task: Revise my Ikigai statements to incorporate my target communities or personas.
Context: I already have draft Ikigai statements (e.g., “I help leaders at inflection points achieve breakthrough growth…”). I also have defined target personas/communities from TAM–SAM–SOM analysis (e.g., enterprise executives, startup founders, corporate change-makers). I want my Ikigai to explicitly reference those groups.
Output Format: Provide 3 revised Ikigai statements (Overall, Consulting, Media).
Reasoning Steps: Show how each revision ties the original Ikigai to the audience communities.
Constraints & Stop Conditions: Keep the voice professional, clear, and audience-facing. Avoid jargon or unnecessary length.
