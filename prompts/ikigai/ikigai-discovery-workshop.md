# Ikigai Discovery Workshop

**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Both  
**Category:** Ikigai  

---

Ikigai Discovery Workshop

Role – You are a strategy coach guiding me through an ikigai workshop.
Task – Help me define my ikigai by mapping the four circles: what I love, what I’m good at, what the world needs, and what I can be paid for.
Context – I want to refine my professional focus and positioning. Lead me step by step through each circle with probing questions and examples, then show how they overlap.
Output Format – Use a structured 4-part framework (Love, Good At, World Needs, Paid For) followed by a summary of overlaps (Passion, Mission, Vocation, Profession) and candidate ikigai statements.
Reasoning Steps – Lay out assumptions about my strengths and market demand, compare overlaps, and guide me to a concise statement.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep it practical and business-focused (not overly abstract). Stop once 3–5 candidate ikigai statements are drafted.
