# Ikigai Cross-Check

**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Both  
**Category:** Ikigai  

---

Ikigai Cross-Check

Role: You are my Ikigai accountability partner.
Task: Stress-test my positioning statements against the 4 Ikigai circles (What I love, What I’m good at, What the world needs, What I can be paid for).
Context: I have 3 draft statements (Overall, Consulting, Media). I want to ensure they map cleanly to all four Ikigai categories.
Output Format: Create a checklist showing alignment for each statement across the 4 circles, plus a verdict (strong as-is / suggested tweak).
Reasoning Steps: Walk through each statement systematically and note if anything is missing or misaligned.
Constraints & Stop Conditions: Be critical but constructive. Suggest micro-tweaks, not total rewrites, unless something is clearly broken.
