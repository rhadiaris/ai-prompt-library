# ICP Master Prompts


---

Below are my **core prompts**, in the exact style I run (Tree-of-Thought branching, one question at a time, A–F answers).

---

## 1) ICP Discovery Core Prompt (Tree-of-Thought)

**Purpose:** Narrow to *one* specific hypothetical person (your ICP).

**Prompt template:**

* “We’re defining ONE ideal customer (a single person). I’ll ask one broad question at a time, with A–F answers. Pick one option (or ‘F: other’). We’ll keep narrowing until we can describe them precisely enough to write posts that feel like they’re ‘for them.’
  **Question:** Which direction should we narrow first?”

(Then I present A–F options and only one question.)

---

## 2) Branch-Narrowing Prompt (One path at a time)

**Purpose:** Keep the ToT structure without listing full branches.

**Prompt template:**

* “You chose **[their selection]**. Let’s narrow one level deeper.
  **Question:** Which of these best matches your ideal person?”

(A–F options, always mutually exclusive where possible.)

---

## 3) Precision Lock Prompt (force “one person” specificity)

**Purpose:** Prevent vague ICPs like “founders” or “marketing leaders.”

**Prompt template:**

* “I’m going to force this to ONE person. Pick the closest:
  A) exact role + seniority
  B) exact industry
  C) exact company stage/size
  D) exact job-to-be-done
  E) exact pain they’ll pay to solve
  F) other (tell me in 5–10 words)”

---

## 4) Pain-to-Purchase Prompt (what triggers action)

**Purpose:** Identify the “buying moment” and emotional driver.

**Prompt template:**

* “What’s happening when they *finally* decide they need help?
  A) revenue plateau
  B) pipeline uncertainty
  C) time scarcity / burnout
  D) competitive pressure
  E) leadership/board expectations
  F) other”

---

## 5) Content Resonance Prompt (what they actually stop scrolling for)

**Purpose:** Match content style to consumption habits.

**Prompt template:**

* “What makes them pause on LinkedIn?
  A) contrarian take
  B) tactical how-to
  C) case study with numbers
  D) frameworks/checklists
  E) personal story with lesson
  F) other”

---

## 6) Objection Mapping Prompt (why they *don’t* engage/buy)

**Purpose:** Build content that neutralizes objections.

**Prompt template:**

* “Why would they ignore you even if you’re right?
  A) ‘sounds generic’
  B) ‘not credible enough’
  C) ‘too complex / no time’
  D) ‘already tried that’
  E) ‘wrong timing/budget’
  F) other”

---

## 7) Differentiation Prompt (your unfair advantage)

**Purpose:** Turn your experience into positioning.

**Prompt template:**

* “What’s the most defensible thing about your approach?
  A) unique method/framework
  B) niche proof (specific audience results)
  C) speed of implementation
  D) clarity/simplicity
  E) network/distribution
  F) other”

---

## 8) “ICP Output” Synthesis Prompt (final profile)

**Purpose:** Produce the final one-person ICP + content rules.

**Prompt template:**

* “Synthesize everything into ONE hypothetical person with:
* name, age range, location/timezone (if relevant)
* role, company size, industry, seniority
* goals (12 months), fears, constraints
* top 3 pains + triggers
* what they believe now (and what you need to shift)
* how they consume LinkedIn + preferred formats
* keywords, topics, and ‘instant turn-offs’
* content angles: hook → insight → action → CTA”

---

## 9) Post Generator Prompt (once ICP is locked)

**Purpose:** Create ICP-native posts reliably.

**Prompt template:**

* “Write **[N]** LinkedIn posts for **[ICP persona]**.
  Constraints:
* tone: **[tone]**
* length: **[short/medium/long]**
* format mix: **[how-to/story/framework/case study]**
* include: hook + 1 core insight + example + practical step + soft CTA
* avoid: **[turn-offs]**
* each post targets one pain/objection”

---

## 10) Comment/DM Conversion Prompt (engagement → conversation)

**Purpose:** Turn attention into inbound.

**Prompt template:**

* “Create:
  A) 10 comment templates that add value (no pitch)
  B) 5 DM openers tied to their engagement behavior
  C) 3 follow-up messages that offer a resource
  All aligned to **[ICP persona]** and their top pain.”
