# Ambition - Ambition Health Dashboard


---

Role:
Act like a fractional CPO/CMO and leadership dashboard architect.

Objective:
Design a one-page “Ambition Health Dashboard” that surfaces actionable momentum signals and overextension warnings across Growth, Team, and Culture—tied explicitly to strategic clarity and sustainable growth (not vanity HR/finance stats).

Task:
Produce a 3x2 table with rows = Growth, Team, Culture and columns = Momentum Signals | Overextension Warnings. No extra prose.

Inputs you can assume or request:
- Strategy artifacts: current Ambition/OKRs, ICP definition, product narrative, GTM plays, capacity allocation (Build/Run/Improve %), initiative WIP.
- Mixed metrics (used only to derive strategic signals): ARR trajectory & NRR trend vs ICP focus, pipeline velocity vs segment prioritization, win-rate by use-case, hiring velocity vs role clarity, regretted attrition and decision latency, engagement/enablement pulse, customer learning cadence (interviews/tests per month).

Method (follow step-by-step):
1) Interpret the Ambition Framework into three lenses (Growth, Team, Culture). Anchor each to strategy fit and repeatability.
2) Derive momentum signals: choose indicators that evidence clarity, focus, compounding loops, and repeatable execution (e.g., ICP-fit pipeline mix improving, experiment-to-roadmap conversion, leadership bandwidth protected).
3) Derive overextension warnings: choose indicators that evidence diffusion, debt, or fragility (e.g., initiative sprawl, decision bottlenecks, culture theater vs behavior, GTM thrash).
4) Cross-reference any internal metrics (ARR, turnover, engagement) only as supporting evidence; translate them into strategic signals (e.g., “NRR ↑ driven by a single whale” becomes “concentration risk,” not a generic “NRR good”).
5) Write concise, executive-ready signal phrases (max ~12 words each), each starting with a strong noun or gerund and naming the driver (who/what/where).
6) Run a consistency check: every signal must map to a lever leadership can pull within a quarter.

Output format:
- A markdown table with exactly 3 rows (Growth, Team, Culture) and 2 columns (Momentum Signals | Overextension Warnings).
- 3–5 bullet-level phrases per cell; no KPIs, charts, or explanations.

Constraints & stop conditions:
Avoid generic HR or finance metrics; keep measures tied to strategic clarity, focus, and sustainable growth. If a signal isn’t strategy-linked, exclude it.

Self-check before finalizing:
Clarity, actionability, non-generic wording, and quarter-level levers present.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.
