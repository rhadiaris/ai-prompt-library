# LinkedIn Analysis


---

ChatGPT Atlas turns LinkedIn into your intelligence layer.

Here's 6 ways I use it (with exact prompts):

1. Analyse Your Profile

Ask ChatGPT to break down your About section.

It spots gaps and missed opportunities.

Prompt: “Review my LinkedIn profile, identify gaps, and rewrite my headline for clarity and conversion.”

2. Profile Intelligence

Compares your positioning with top creators.

Shows exactly how to differentiate.

Prompt: "Analyse my LinkedIn profile and tell me how it positions me in the [Insert Niche]."

3. Post Feedback

Grades your post and rewrites weak lines.

Keeps your voice while boosting engagement.

Prompt: "Review my post draft, fix structure and rhythm, and suggest a stronger first line."

4. Engagement Decoder

Maps what hooks and topics get traction.

Reveals what your audience actually wants.

Prompt: "Compare my last 5 posts and show which tone or structure drives the most comments."

5. Prospect Research

Summarises profiles for instant insights.

Makes personalised outreach take seconds.

Prompt: "Analyse this founder's profile and give me 3 authentic ideas to start a conversation."

6. Idea Generation

Reverse-engineers your best content.

Creates new posts from proven winners.

Prompt: "Based on my top-performing posts, suggest 10 new angles on AI storytelling for LinkedIn."
