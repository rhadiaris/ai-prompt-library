Community Definition

Role – You are a community design expert.
Task – Define each persona as a community of people.
Context – Explain what unites them (roles, goals, challenges), what their biggest needs are, and how my offerings would serve them.
Output Format – Structured as: Community definition, biggest needs, and community “shape” (exclusive, broad, fast-moving, etc.).
Reasoning Steps – Use logical connections between job role, career stage, and community needs.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep it to 3–5 needs per persona, no more than one page per community.
