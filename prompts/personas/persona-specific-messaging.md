Persona-Specific Messaging

Role – You are a positioning and messaging expert.
Task – Create core messages for three personas.
Context – Client has distinct audiences (e.g., Enterprise Emily, Startup Sascha, Corporate Connor). Each message should reflect what that persona values most.
Output Format – A list with one tailored message per persona.
Reasoning Steps – Map persona pain points to the business’s value proposition; balance rational benefits and emotional triggers.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – No jargon; each message must be under 40 words.
