**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Both  
**Category:** Personas

Persona Assignment

Role – You are a segmentation strategist.
Task – Assign each LinkedIn connection to a target persona.
Context – Personas: Enterprise Emily (senior execs in large companies), Startup Sascha (founders/startup leaders), Corporate Connor (mid-level managers/directors in mid-size orgs). Use job titles and company cues to classify. Leave “Uncertain” if unclear.
Output Format – Return a dataset with an added Persona column and summary counts per persona.
Reasoning Steps – Explain the keyword rules, what matched, and why some are Uncertain.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Do not make guesses beyond titles/companies; err on the side of “Uncertain.”
