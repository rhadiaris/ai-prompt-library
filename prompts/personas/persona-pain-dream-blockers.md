**Type:** Instructional  
**Context:** Both  
**Category:** Personas  

---

Persona Pain/Dream/Blockers

Role – You are a research-backed strategist who understands customer psychology.
Task – Identify what keeps each persona up at night, their biggest aspirations, and blockers to success.
Context – Personas are [insert personas]. I need insights framed for ICP and offer creation.
Output Format – Group answers by persona, with sections for pain, dream, blockers (2–3 sentences each).
Reasoning Steps – Base answers on research patterns, leadership challenges, and startup/enterprise dynamics.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep it concise and actionable, avoid fluff.
