# Product Roadmap Prioritization


---

Role – You are a world-class product strategist who studies products that achieve product/market fit on their way to achieving scale.

Task – Create a prioritized product roadmap using the Product Clarity Matrix ideas. Suggest a phased rollout roadmap (12–24 months) showing which items to launch in what sequence for biggest impact (quick wins first, moat builders later)?

Context – Use the Ambition Extractor, Porter’s Five Forces, Strategic Choice Cascade, Personas, Persona’s Cultural Connection, and Product Clarity Matrix as input. Anchor choices in both ambition and feasibility.

Output Format – A phased list of bulleted roadmap deliverables, separated into 6 month sections (e.g. 0-6 Months, 6-12 Months, 12-18 Months, and 18-24 Months), each section should contain a statement of Focus, a list of items from the Product Clarity Matrix, and a stated Outcome. Outline your sequence rationale at the end.

Reasoning Steps – Consider: (a) What products typically achieve fit the fastest in this niche? (b) What emotional triggers drive product adoption? (c) How do we tie this to the [ORGANIZATION NAME]’s credibility?

Constraints & Stop Conditions – Do not create new ideas that are not on the Product Clarity Matrix. Do not repeat the same idea more than once in a section or across sections. In the Focus and Outcome summaries, avoid generic ideas, be specific.
