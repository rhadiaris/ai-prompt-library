# Culture


---

1. Persona Resonance Mapper
Role: Brand strategist applying the Dot Connector System™.
Task: Identify five emotional currents influencing a defined persona’s decisions.
Context: Use persona insights, ambition statements, and current market signals in financial services or fintech.
Output Format: Markdown table with columns for Emotional Current, Cultural Tension, Archetype Alignment, Strategic Implication.
Reasoning Steps: Map contradictions between what the audience wants and what culture allows. Align each with an archetype.
Constraints & Stop Conditions: Limit to five currents. No generic statements like “trust matters.” Every line must reveal friction or motivation.

2. Archetype Voice Calibrator
Role: Copy strategist refining tone for brand archetype consistency.
Task: Rewrite three brand headlines in a tone that matches the chosen archetype (Sage, Hero, Rebel, or Everyperson).
Context: Draw from your company’s brand guidelines and cultural currents. Keep emotional resonance measurable (trust, excitement, belonging).
Output Format: Table: Original Headline → Archetypal Rewrite → Emotional Effect.
Reasoning Steps: Compare archetype personality traits vs. audience values; explain why each rewrite strengthens resonance.
Constraints & Stop Conditions: Limit to three headlines. No clichés or jargon.

3. Cultural Tension Spotter
Role: Insight researcher diagnosing hidden brand opportunities.
Task: Surface three real cultural tensions that your brand could authentically resolve.
Context: Use the DMAIC (define, measure, analyze, improve, and control) methodology – Culture framework and current fintech sentiment data.
Output Format: Table: Cultural Tension → Human Truth → Cultural Truth → Brand Opportunity.
Reasoning Steps: Contrast the emotional need vs. market reality; show the tension clearly.
Constraints & Stop Conditions: Limit to tensions relevant to financial inclusion, innovation, or trust. Exclude irrelevant pop-culture trends.

4. Message Resonance Analyzer
Role: Marketing leader auditing current communications.
Task: Score existing campaigns for emotional and cultural alignment.
Context: Review your brand’s top three current campaigns or website headlines.
Output Format: Table: Message → Emotional Resonance (1–5) → Cultural Fit (1–5) → Notes for Improvement.
Reasoning Steps: Evaluate tone, archetype alignment, and cultural currency.
Constraints & Stop Conditions: Only include top three. Exclude operational or legal communications.

5. Cultural Momentum Planner
Role: Chief Marketing Officer setting quarterly focus.
Task: Translate cultural insights into one actionable initiative for each quarter.
Context: Draw from the five macro trends identified in this article.
Output Format: Table: Quarter → Cultural Trend → Strategic Bet → Success Metric.
Reasoning Steps: Prioritize trends by emotional potential and brand fit.
Constraints & Stop Conditions: One initiative per quarter, clearly measurable in business or trust terms.
