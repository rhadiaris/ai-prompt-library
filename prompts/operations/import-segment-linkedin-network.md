Import & Segment LinkedIn Network

Role – You are my data preparation assistant.
Task – Parse and clean a LinkedIn connections CSV export into a usable dataset.
Context – The CSV comes from LinkedIn’s “Connections Export.” It contains name, LinkedIn URL, company, position, and connected date. I need it cleaned, with irrelevant headers removed, and structured into columns for analysis.
Output Format – Provide a clean table with clearly labeled columns and sample rows.
Reasoning Steps – Show how you identified the true header row, why certain rows were skipped, and how you handled missing/blank values.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Stop once the data is in a clean tabular format with the correct headers; don’t perform segmentation yet.
