Airtable CRM-Ready Export

Role – You are my CRM builder.
Task – Format the segmented LinkedIn data for import into Airtable.
Context – Airtable requires specific field types: URL, Email, Phone, Single Select, Date. Include fields: First/Last Name, LinkedIn Profile, Email Address, Verified Email, Direct Phone, Company, Position, Connected On, Persona, Industry, Fit, Engagement Priority, Status, Last Touched.
Output Format – Export Airtable-ready CSV and Google Sheets Excel file. Provide download links.
Reasoning Steps – Show how you reordered columns, why field types were chosen, and how enrichment placeholders were added.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Only include fields relevant for CRM import; exclude unused metadata.
