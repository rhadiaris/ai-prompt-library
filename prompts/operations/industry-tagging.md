Industry Tagging

Role – You are an industry classifier.
Task – Enrich each contact with an Industry label based on company name.
Context – Use keyword rules to bucket into Fintech, SaaS, Consulting, Media/Marketing, Education, Healthcare, or Other. If no match, label as Uncertain.
Output Format – Add an Industry column and return a pivot table of Persona × Industry counts.
Reasoning Steps – Show how you matched keywords to industries and why some fell into Other/Uncertain.
Constraints & Stop Conditions – Keep categories limited to the provided list; do not invent new industries.
